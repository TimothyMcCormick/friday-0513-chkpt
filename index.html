VOS ROASTERS

Third wave coffee - a movement in coffee marketing emphasizing high quality. Our beans are sourced from individual farms and are roasted more lightly to bring out their distinctive flavors.

Sourced from individual farms.

Lightly roasted for fullest flavor.

Wide range of flavor profiles from fruity to hints of cocoa. Methodically roasted in a way that best highlights the natural nuances and complexities of each coffee bean.

  • Ethiopian Blend

    Profile: Blackberry, Strawberry, Candy Sweet

  • Guatemalan Blend

    Profile - Cherry, Black Currant, Milk Chocolate

  • Colombian Blend

    Profile - Raspberry, Orange, Green Apple, Caramel

  • Brazilian Blend

    Profile - Strawberry, Milk Chocolate, Lemon
